<span>Gail Bromage</span>
<p><span>Gail Bromage is a quality director who specializes in quality management systems and regulatory readiness. Gail has been with Labcorp since May of 2000. &nbsp;Gail’s focus is on strengthening the quality management systems in oncology, pathology and women’s health and genetics. &nbsp;Gail believes that Quality’s role is supporting both the internal and external customer while putting our patients at the center of all we do.</span></p><p><span>Gail previously worked in the clinical laboratory field as a cytogenetic technologist lead specializing in genetics and oncology after graduating from Grove City College in Pennsylvania. &nbsp;She holds certifications from the American Society for Quality as a certified quality auditor (CQA) and quality manager of operational excellence (CMQOE), is a Six Sigma Green Belt and is licensed as a cytogenetic technologist (CG) by the American Society of Clinical Pathologists.</span></p><p><span>When she is not working, Gail can be found reading, taking hikes or walks on the beach, traveling and relishing time with her family</span><br />&nbsp;</p><span><span>bfajardo</span></span>
